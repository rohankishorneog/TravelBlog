About

I have always been captivated by the allure of the great outdoors and the thrill of discovery. It was on a trip to the mountains at the age of 19 that I first discovered my love for offroading and touring on bikes. The rough terrain and the sense of freedom that came with tackling it sparked a fire within me that has never died.

Since then, I have had the opportunity to travel to some of the most beautiful and remote places on earth, each one a new adventure full of unexpected wonders. From the serene beauty of the forests to the rugged wildness of the desert, I have experienced it all and my love for exploration only grows stronger with each passing day.

My name is Rohan Kishor Neog and I am a 22 year old adventurer at heart. I am excited to share my stories and experiences with you through this travel blog and to possibly inspire you to seek out your own adventures and create memories that will last a lifetime. Thank you for joining me on this journey.
